About the Game

Broken Bridge Online is an MMORPG in development focused on two warring kingdoms in an apocalyptic world invaded by beasts. Its strength is the PvP between the two largest nations, Serolf and Nidraj, who seek to control the market and dominate the different continents of the world. Initially, you can choose to belong to either of these factions to bring glory and honor to your nation through action-packed PvP battles in zones available at all hours, or remain neutral.

Main Features

Classes

Over 8 selectable classes, each with unique specializations and combat mechanics.

Nations

Two warring nations offering intense PvP warfare at all times.

Equipment Sets

Wide variety of gear sets providing solutions for any combat situation.

Combat Mechanics

Advanced combat systems for both PvP and PvE, with diverse strategies based on stat choices.

Government System

Become the most powerful player in your kingdom and control wars/diplomacy for your nation.

Boss Battles

Numerous bosses with unique combat mechanics and challenges.

Events

Automatic daily, weekly and monthly events including clan wars, nation wars, invasions and RvR content.

Management

Players can manage individual and group content including player housing, guild castles and cities.

Customization

Deep character customization including facial features, skin tones, hair styles and over 3 playable races.

Frequently Asked Questions

When will the game be released?

We’re in early development, so no exact date yet. Follow our Discord for updates!

Will BBO be on PC or mobile?

PC first, with Android planned for later. Aiming for Steam release too!

Is the game free?

Yes! Free to play with cosmetic microtransactions (no pay-to-win).

What gameplay can I expect?

Both! PvE (quests, bosses) and PvP (arenas, guild battles). Plus a main story + open world.

Can I join the beta test?

Yes! A closed beta is planned for mid-2024. Join our Discord for updates.
